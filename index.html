    Ari (ahr-ee), noun. The creator of this website.

    A self critical nerd who tries too hard or not at all.

    Whenever I'm not coding I enjoy reading, studying linguistics, playing old games and digging up anything wierd and wonderful. I like hot coffee, watercolor paints, the smell of the earth after a thunderstorm, the works of Möebius and origami animals. I dislike cold coffee, dark chocolate, flat root beer and broken pencils.
