Deer Mask Archive

Ari (ɑːri), noun. The creator of this website.

Overcaffeinated and contemplating the absurdity of it all.

This is a personal site for my random projects, thoughts, etc. It's primarily just an excuse to work on my HTML skills and force myself to keep up to date on my other hobbies, but feel free to have a look around. I have an interest in old games, software emulation, cassette and VHS tapes, CDS, obsolete technology and anything wierd and wonderful.

This website has been under perpetual construction since October of 2024, and I have resigned myself to the fate of eternally designing and redesigning these pages until death do us part. What began as a simple "I wonder if I could do that?" turned into me spending more hours than I would ever like to admit reading through HTML documentation and coming up with new and interesting layouts. At this point the website has gone under at least 7 different redesigns, 1 server change, and has traveled between multiple computers and hard drives.

These days the website demands less of me. I have less free time than I did two years ago, and the tinkering process is much more sporadic, akin to remembering an email you should have written many weeks ago.
