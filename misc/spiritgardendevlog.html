Pitch/Manifesto:

Spirit Garden is a 2D exploration puzzle game with a focus on atmosphere, immersion, storytelling, and tactile interactive elements. The player journeys into the strange shores of spirits, which explore the ideas of ecology, nostalgia, and memories, as well as creative expression and the nature of fulfillment.

May 8th - Created a player character with basic movement, a tileset environment, and rudimentary collision. Window size and resolution were also taken care of.

May 9-10 - More robust 8 dimensional movement, y sorting enabled. Setup git repo.

May 12 - Scene transition script, camera system, occlusion shader for when behind objects
